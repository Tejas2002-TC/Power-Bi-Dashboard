📊 Power BI Sales Dashboard – Balaji Traders & Namkeen
🚀 Project Overview

This interactive Power BI Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and payment trends for Balaji Traders & Namkeen. The dashboard enables business users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions through intuitive visualizations and dynamic filters.

✨ Key Features
📈 Sales Performance Analysis

Track total sales amount, quantity sold, profit, and average order value (AOV).
Monitor business performance across different time periods.

🌍 State-wise Sales Insights
Analyze revenue contribution from various states.
Identify top-performing regions for business growth.

📦 Category & Sub-Category Analysis
View quantity distribution across product categories.
Understand profit contribution by individual sub-categories.

💳 Payment Mode Analytics
Examine customer payment preferences including:
• UPI
• COD
• Debit Card
• Credit Card
• EMI

👥 Customer Insights
Identify top customers based on purchase amount.
Support customer segmentation and retention strategies.

📅 Monthly Profit Tracking
Visualize profit trends over different months.
Detect seasonal patterns and business opportunities.

🔍 Interactive Filters
Quarter-wise filtering (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
State-level analysis for focused insights.
